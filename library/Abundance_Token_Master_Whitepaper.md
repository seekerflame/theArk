# Part 1: Governance & Foundation

## 1.1 Constitution

### Preamble

We, the builders of the future, establish this Constitution to secure a regenerative, abundant, and equitable civilization. The Abundance Token (AT) is not merely a currency but a tool for social and ecological transformation, anchored in real-world value and dedicated to the well-being of all life.

### Article I: Core Principles

1.  **Regenerative Value**: Anchored in Labor, Ecological Regeneration, and Physical Production.
2.  **Anti-Inflationary Nature**: Supply strictly regulated by value creation.
3.  **Open Source Ethos**: All knowledge remains open and accessible.

### Article II: Rights of Participants

1.  **Right to Participation**: Fair compensation for contribution.
2.  **Right to Transparency**: Verifiable governance and ledgers.
3.  **Right to Fork**: Power to diverge if the system fails the community.

## 1.2 Governance Model

### Structure

1.  **The Council of Builders (Meritocratic)**: Contributors who propose upgrades. Voting weight based on "Proof of Contribution".
2.  **The Community Assembly (Democratic)**: Token holders who veto/approve. One person, one vote (or quadratic).
3.  **The Algorithmic Guardian (Automated)**: Smart contracts enforcing hard rules (inflation caps).

### Decision Making

Proposal -> Review -> Voting (Council + Assembly) -> Execution.

# Part 2: Economics

## 2.1 Tokenomics

### Supply Mechanism

- **Minting**: Triggered by verified Proof of Value (Labor, Eco-data, Machines).
    - *Formula*: `New_Tokens = (Hours * Base_Rate) * Quality_Multiplier`
- **Burning**: Tokens spent on "Commons" rent or resources may be burned or recycled.

### Value Anchors

- **Labor**: 1 AT ≈ 1 Hour of Basic Labor (The "Labor Anchor").
- **Energy**: 1 AT ≈ X kWh renewable energy.
- **Food**: 1 AT ≈ X Calories organic produce.

### Anti-Speculation

- **Reputation Score**: Non-transferable score tracking contribution.
- **Vesting**: Large mints locked over time.

## 2.2 Value Proposition

- **Individual**: Fair pay for regenerative work; security independent of fiat.
- **Community**: Local wealth circulation; funding GVCS.
- **World**: A scalable blueprint for post-scarcity.

## 2.3 Physical Coin Blueprint

- **Materials**: Recycled precious metals (Silver/Gold) or E-waste Copper.
- **Tech**: Embedded NFC/RFID chip storing public key/cert.
- **Design**: Bi-metallic. "In Soil We Trust".
- **Tiers**: Copper (Labor), Silver (Skill), Gold (Stewardship).

# Part 3: Technology

## 3.1 Tech Stack

- **Ledger**: DAG (IOTA/Hedera) for feeless, scalable, offline-capable transactions.
- **Contracts**: Rust or Solidity.
- **Identity**: W3C DIDs (Polygon ID) for privacy-preserving reputation.
- **Frontend**: React Native (Mobile) + Next.js (Web).
- **IoT**: ESP32/LoRaWAN for eco-sensors.

## 3.2 Hosting

- **Seed Node**: High-spec archive node at HQ.
- **Village Node**: Ruggedized edge server (Pi/NUC) at each campus.
- **Personal Node**: Light wallet on phones.
- **Network**: P2P Mesh (Starlink/LoRa) for resilience.

## 3.3 Minting Protocols

1.  **Labor (Proof of Work)**: Log time + GPS/Peer Verify -> Mint.
2.  **Eco (Proof of Regeneration)**: Sensor Data + Signature -> Mint.
3.  **Machine (Proof of Production)**: Build GVCS + Audit -> Mint (Vested).
