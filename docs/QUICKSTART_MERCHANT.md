## Why Post Quests?

Get help with tasks **without paying cash**. Workers earn AT (Abundance Token), and you get work done.

---

## How to Post a Quest (2 Minutes)

### 1. Open The Ark

Log in with your merchant account.

### 2. Tap "Post Quest"

Fill out:

- **Title**: "Wash dishes for 30 min"
- **AT Reward**: 3 AT
- **Category**: Kitchen / Cleaning / Delivery / Social

### 3. Wait for a Worker

Someone will claim it. Verify they completed it. Done!

## Why This Works

- **Workers**: They're bored, looking for something to do. You give them purpose.
- **You**: Get free labor (no cash). Just verify their work.
- **Economy**: AT circulates. Everyone wins.
