# Content Moderation Policy (v1.0)

**Purpose**: Prevent violence and harm while preserving freedom  
**Philosophy**: Minimum viable moderation  

---

## What IS Moderated (The Red Lines)

### 1. Direct Threats of Violence

- ❌ "Help me hurt [person]"
- ❌ "Looking for someone to [violent act]"
- ❌ Organizing physical attacks

**Action**: Instant ban, report to authorities

### 2. Illegal Content (Per US Law)

- ❌ Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- ❌ Human trafficking coordination
- ❌ Credible terrorist threats

**Action**: Report to NCMEC/FBI, ban user

### 3. Scams & Fraud

- ❌ "Send AT, get 10x back!"
- ❌ Fake verification schemes
- ❌ Ponzi/pyramid schemes

**Action**: Flag, community review, potential ban

---

## What is NOT Moderated

### Political Speech

- ✅ Offensive opinions
- ✅ Controversial ideas
- ✅ Criticism of government

### Economic Activity

- ✅ Selling legal goods (even if you disagree)
- ✅ Competitive pricing
- ✅ "Controversial" services (legal sex work, etc.)

### Profanity & Insults

- ✅ Swearing
- ✅ Mean comments
- ✅ Heated arguments

**Philosophy**: Free humans use free speech. Block/mute exists for a reason.

---

## The Process

### Step 1: Community Reporting

- Any user can flag content
- Costs 0.1 AT to prevent spam
- Refunded if flag is upheld

### Step 2: Oracle Review

- 5 independent oracles vote
- Must be 4/5 to take action
- Decision is public (pseudonymous)

### Step 3: Action Taken

- **Warning** (first offense, grey area)
- **Suspension** (7 days, second offense)
- **Ban** (permanent, violence/illegal)

### Step 4: Appeals

- Banned user can appeal to community court
- 9 randomly selected users vote
- 7/9 required to overturn

### Step 5: Transparency

- All bans logged publicly
- Reason stated (no secret moderation)
- Appeals process visible

---

## The Safeguards

### Against False Positives

- High threshold (4/5 oracles)
- Appeals process
- Compensation for wrongful ban

### Against Censorship

- No "misinformation" bans (truth is subjective)
- No political bias (oracles are random)
- Fork rights (if you disagree, leave)

### Against Capture

- Oracles rotated randomly
- No permanent moderator class
- Community can vote out corrupt oracles

---

*"Ban violence. Permit speech. Trust the community."*
