# Provenance Case Studies: Trust Through Transparency

**Core Insight**: Every seller wants to prove their work is REAL. Provenance = proof.

---

## Case Study 1: Rudy's Hot Dog Stand

### The Problem

- No way to prove quality
- Competing with chains (McDonald's, etc.)
- Can't afford traditional marketing

### The Provenance Solution

```
Step 1: YOU take professional photos
Step 2: Photos uploaded to Rudy's Ark profile
Step 3: QR code on cart links to photos
Step 4: Customers see: "This is what you're buying"
```

### What's Tracked

- ✅ Menu photos (visual proof of quality)
- ✅ Setup process (shows cleanliness)
- ✅ Customer reviews (linked to verified purchases)
- ✅ Hours of operation (transparency)

### What's NOT Tracked

- ❌ Customer faces (privacy)
- ❌ Personal data (no SSN, no address)
- ❌ Location tracking (unless opt-in for delivery)

### Trust Built

- "I can SEE the hot dog before I buy"
- "Real customers verified this (not fake reviews)"
- "Rudy has been here for X months (reputation)"

## The Universal Template

For ANY seller:

```
1. CAPTURE the work (photos, video, timestamps)
2. VERIFY with witnesses (clients, inspectors, oracles)
3. RECORD on ledger (immutable, timestamped)
4. SHARE selectively (you choose what's public)
```

### What Gets Tracked (Always)

- Work performed (photos, descriptions)
- Verification (signatures, reviews)
- Transactions (payments, deliveries)

### What NEVER Gets Tracked

- Personal identity (unless you choose)
- Real names (pseudonymous wallets)
- Location (unless relevant to service)

## The Answer to Your Question

> **"Is this just global surveillance?"**

**No.** It's the opposite. Here's why:

1. **YOU own the data** (your photos, your key, your wallet)
2. **YOU choose what to share** (public profile vs private transactions)
3. **YOU profit from trust** (better reputation = more sales)
4. **No corporate middleman** (no Yelp, no Etsy, no Amazon extracting value)

**Surveillance = They watch you to control you.**  
**Provenance = You show your work to earn trust.**
